Rock Climbing has been my main hobby for the past 9 years. When climbing, I keep my approach simple. If I am inspired and know I might have some fun or the best experience ever, then I will attempt to climb. The line worth climbing must have the following:

  • strong resistance to human contact (not choss)
  • beautiful mineral composition
  • distinctive features

I don't consider myself an athlete due to my less than average genetic limits. However, I have been fortunate enough to be outfitted by an incredible outdoor apparel company, Hippy tree and supplied with shoes and safety gear by Mad Rock Climbing. These two companies have supported me for over the past 5 years.

  • Sport Climbed up to 5.13c
  • Trad Climbed up to 5.11a
